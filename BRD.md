# EIGHTLINES FLEET PRIVATE LIMITED
## Business Requirement Document (BRD)
### Fleet Financial ERP - Core Business Modules Blueprint

**Prepared By:** Numer Saqlain M (Chief Financial & Business Reviewer), Syed Fardeen (Founder & CEO)
**Date:** July 1, 2026
**Document Version:** v1.0

This document serves as the comprehensive Business Requirement Document (BRD) detailing the operational workflows, multi-tier financial rules, asset tracking states, and role-based access configurations required to complete the remaining core modules of the Fleet Financial ERP.

---

### 1. Complete Business Workflow & Asset Architecture
The ERP must natively support two distinct fleet business models under a unified operational asset lifecycle: the **Owned Fleet (11 Vehicles)** framework and the **Co-Hosted / Client Fleet (4 Vehicles)** framework.

- **[Vehicle Sourcing & Onboarding]** -> **[Admin Approval Gateway: Mohammed Azam]** -> **[Dynamic Platform / Offline Market Listing]** -> **[Booking Lifecycle Execution: Shaik Afnan Sabil]**
- **[Financial Settlement Engine Processing]**
    - Owned Fleet: Deduct Expenses/EMI -> Owner P&L
    - Client Fleet: Deduct Liabilities -> Performance Split (20-30%) Deduction -> Net Client Payout -> Distribute GST/TDS

**Asset Multi-Platform State Machine**
- **Simultaneous Availability Pool:** Vehicles must support a concurrent "Shared Availability Pool" state. A single vehicle asset must be capable of remaining open for active bookings on Zoomcar, Revv, Bharat Cars, and Offline/Corporate traditional channels simultaneously.
- **Dynamic Re-assignment:** The system must allow immediate switching of an asset's primary platform designation based on real-time market demand and localized platform yield metrics.

### 2. Vehicle Onboarding & Asset Control
- **Onboarding Gatekeeper:** Adding a new vehicle asset into the live fleet is restricted behind an authorization gateway. Only Mohammed Azam (MD) possesses the administrative permissions required to validate, approve, and activate a vehicle profile to a live state.
- **Asset Segmentation:** The schema must hard-tag every asset upon creation with a mandatory binary metadata flag: `OWNED` or `CO-HOSTED_CLIENT`.

### 3. Platform & Booking Management Engine

**Platform Configuration Matrix**
The system tracks primary platforms while providing sub-category tracking for doorstep vs. guest fulfillment types.

| Main Platform | Tracking Categories | Revenue Calculation Model | Payout Cycle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Zoomcar | Self Guest, Doorstep | Booking (Commission + GST Gross + TDS) | 15-Day Net Settlement |
| Revv | Self Guest, Doorstep | Booking (Commission + GST Gross + TDS) | 15-Day Net Settlement |
| Bharat Cars | Self Guest, Doorstep | Booking (Commission + GST Gross + TDS) | 15-Day Net Settlement |
| Offline | Self Guest, Doorstep | Fixed Daily Rate + Itemized Incidentals | Immediate / Per-Booking |

**Booking Record Schema**
Every booking record generated by Shaik Afnan Sabil (Journal Manager) must enforce the mandatory entry of the following structural parameters before database persistence:
- **Core Information:** Customer Name, Verified Contact Number, Start Date/Time, End Date/Time, Starting Kilometers, Ending Kilometers.
- **Inspection Media:** Obligatory image upload attachments detailing the vehicle's structural and aesthetic state for both Pre-check and Post-check stages.
- **Itemized Offline Add-ons:** Specialized ledger inputs to capture direct offline incidentals: Fastag, Fuel, Incidents/Traffic Violations, Washing, Cancellation Fees, Late Returns, Co-driver additions, Structural Damages, Doorstep Delivery fees, and Miscellaneous.

*Booking Closure Enforce Rule:* A booking record is programmatically marked as Complete only when the final payment clears and checkout operations finish. Upon shifting to this state, the entire transaction row is permanently locked and strictly immutable across all system user tiers.

### 4. Revenue Flow & Split Architecture

**Co-Hosted (Client) Fleet Logic**
- **Performance-Based Dynamic Split:** The revenue share engine must accommodate a shifting corporate retention rate of 20% to 30%. This split ratio must dynamically calculate based on the individual asset's historical or current-month gross performance tiering.
- **Compliance Deduction Layer:** The financial engine must execute clean accounting deductions for active GST and TDS on the gross client share before releasing the net cash settlement or generating payout ledgers.

**Owned Fleet Logic**
- **Fixed Liability Routing:** For the 11 owned fleet assets registered across varying internal entities, the financial engine must automatically execute individual vehicle cost-clearing, routing designated EMIs and individual net vehicle profits directly to their respective registered owner financial profiles.

### 5. Expense, Outstanding Liabilities, & Maintenance Workflows

**Expense Matrix & Approvals**
- **Operational Approver:** 100% of day-to-day operational expenses (such as quick repairs, fuel top-ups, or tolls) must route directly to Shaik Afnan Sabil for digital evaluation and explicit approval before ledger reconciliation.
- **Cost Center Isolation:** All expenses must bind directly to a explicit Vehicle ID. The database architecture will not recognize generic, unassigned platform-level expenses.
- **Liability Automation:** Recurring asset obligations (Monthly EMIs, Insurance Renewals, Road Tax, Office Rent) must automatically compile under Outstanding Liabilities on the 1st day of their active cycle.
- **State Transition Transaction:** Upon marking an outstanding liability as "Paid", the financial engine must execute an atomic transaction moving the item to the active month's itemized Expense ledger for P&L tracking.

**Maintenance & Vendor Module**
- **Trigger Metrics:** Preventative maintenance and regular servicing schedules must trigger strictly based on Kilometers Driven.
- **Component & Warranty Tracking:** The asset ledger must record serialized logs for high-wear components: Engine Oil, Tyres, Batteries, Brake Pads, Suspension Systems, Filters, Clutch Assemblies, and Air Conditioning. If a tracked component fails or requires replacement inside its active warranty timeframe, the system must trigger a high-priority warranty warning.
- **Data Permanence:** Completed maintenance tickets are locked from subsequent deletion or archival modification.
- **Accountability Flagging:** Every logged service event requires a mandatory binary choice checkbox to determine liability:
    - *Routine Service:* Automatically billed to Client/Co-host account balance.
    - *Breakdown Repair:* Absorbed as Company Operational Expense.

2.  **Net Profit after Client Splits:** Retained company earnings after distributing co-host splits.
3.  **Current Fleet Utilization %:** Programmatic calculation of (Active Booking Assets / Total Available Fleet).
4.  **Active Breakdowns:** Operational alerts displaying non-revenue generating assets undergoing repairs.

### 8. System Invariants
- **Invariant 1:** A finalized, checked-out booking state cannot be modified, edited, or deleted by any user account, regardless of access permission level.
- **Invariant 2:** All financial split parameters, tax allocations, and yield metrics must run exclusively through the centralized isolated Financial Engine to prevent operational drift.
- **Invariant 3:** Completed maintenance history and localized component logs are entirely immutable.
